Chicken Alfredo

Description

Quick recipe to make a tasty chicken alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 pound linguine
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce
  • 1 pack of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 can of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup milk

Steps

  1. In a pot, add water and lightly salt, then bring to a boil. Cook noodles until tender. Drain well.
  2. While noodles are cooking, in a saucepan over medium heat, place chicken, sauce, frozen vegetables, mushrooms, and milk. Cook and stir until vegetables are tender.
  3. Pour chicken sauce over cooked noodles and serve.
